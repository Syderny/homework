1) **证**<br>

&emsp;&emsp;原码 + 补码 = (1 0000 0000)<sub>2</sub> mod (1 0000 0000)<sub>2</sub> = 0	&emsp;(1)<br>
&emsp;&emsp;原码 + 反码 = (1111 1111)<sub>2</sub> = -1	&emsp;(2)<br>
由(1) - (2)得<br>
&emsp;&emsp;补码 = 反码 + 1	&emsp;(3)<br>
